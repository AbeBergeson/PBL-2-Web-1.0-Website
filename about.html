About Us:

Cardinal Book Store is commited to making the book buying experience as easy and enjoyable as possible.
Based in Muncie, Indiana, we are a business home grown here at Ball State University.
We pride ourselves on our customer service and dedication to ensuring students have the resources they need to succeed academically.
Our friendly staff are always ready to assist you in finding the right textbooks and supplies for your courses.
Thank you for choosing Cardinal Book Store for your academic needs!
